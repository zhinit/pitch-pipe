# FR245 App Memory Capacity — Forum Discussion

Source: https://forums.garmin.com/sports-fitness/running-multisport/f/forerunner-245-series/229152/app-memory-capacity
Fetched: 2026-06-14

## Memory Limits

The Forerunner 245 can hold 32 apps maximum, where apps include watch faces,
widgets, and data fields all counting toward this limit.

## Storage Capacity

One user reported finding detailed specs in Garmin Connect: "apps 0 of 32, and
capacity of 4.0MB" for their device.

## Key Insight

The main constraint is the 32-app count ceiling rather than individual app
sizes. Users can check their remaining capacity through Garmin Connect's
Connect IQ section on their computer.

The thread notes this applies to the 245 Music model; specifications may vary
slightly for the standard 245 model.
