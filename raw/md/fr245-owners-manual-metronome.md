# Forerunner 245/245 Music Owner's Manual — Using the Metronome

Source: https://www8.garmin.com/manuals/webhelp/forerunner245/EN-US/GUID-7C3A8FE0-035D-43D2-8E5E-6B0FC75DD00C.html
Fetched: 2026-06-14

## Purpose

The metronome helps runners "improve your performance by training at a faster,
slower, or more consistent cadence."

## How It Works

Users activate the feature through the watch settings menu. It "starts
automatically" once a run begins. During activity, pressing UP or DOWN displays
the metronome screen.

## Sound Method

The feature "plays tones at a steady rhythm." The manual does not specify
whether these are produced through a beeper, speaker, or other audio mechanism.

## Available Settings

1. **Beats Per Minute** — "enter a value based on the cadence you want to
   maintain"
2. **Alert Frequency** — "customize the frequency of the beats"
3. **Sounds** — "customize the metronome tone and vibration"

A Preview option lets users "listen to the metronome feature before you run."

## Limitations

"This feature is not available for all activities."
