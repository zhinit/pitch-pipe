## Sound Options

The device offers four configurable sound settings:

1. **Key Tones** — turn the key tones on and off
2. **Alert Tones** — enable alert tone functionality
3. **Vibration** — turn device vibration on and off
4. **Key Vibe** — enable vibration when you select a key

The documentation does not specify whether the device contains a beeper,
speaker, or both. It references "key tones" and "alert tones" without
detailing the underlying audio hardware.
