# Forerunner 245/245 Music — Device Overview (Buttons)

Source: https://www8.garmin.com/manuals/webhelp/forerunner245/EN-US/GUID-03BA40CB-0D9C-46DA-A547-6E32A04D5BAD.html
Fetched: 2026-06-14

## Button Layout

The device has five physical buttons.

### Left Side (top to bottom)

1. **LIGHT** — Select to turn the device on. Select to turn the backlight on
   and off. Hold to view the controls menu.

2. **UP** — Scroll through widgets, data screens, options, and settings. Hold
   to display the menu.

3. **DOWN** — Scroll through widgets, data screens, options, and settings. Hold
   to open the music controls.

### Right Side (top to bottom)

4. **START/STOP** — Select to start and stop the activity timer. Select to
   choose an option or to acknowledge a message.

5. **BACK** — Select to return to the previous screen. Hold to record a lap
   during an activity.

## Notes

- The device is button-only; it does not have a touchscreen.
- A labeled front-view diagram with numbered callouts accompanies these
  descriptions in the manual.
