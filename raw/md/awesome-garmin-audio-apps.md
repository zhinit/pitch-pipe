# Awesome Garmin — Audio and Timer Apps

Source: https://github.com/bombsimon/awesome-garmin
Fetched: 2026-06-14

Curated list of Garmin apps. Entries relevant to audio, tones, or timers
(which typically use tone/vibration alerts):

## Audio Content Providers

- **SubMusic** — https://github.com/memen45/SubMusic
  Sync music and podcasts from SubSonic or Ampache server. Stars: 165.

## Timer and Interval Apps

- **WorkoutTimer** — https://github.com/xtruan/WorkoutTimer
  Workout timer for Connect IQ. Stars: 10.

- **TempoBPM** — https://github.com/xtruan/TempoBPM
  Tap tempo BPM calculator. Stars: 2. (Does not play tones; measures tap
  tempo only.)

- **EggTimer** — https://github.com/pedlarstudios/EggTimer
  Simple timer for Garmin devices. Stars: 9.

- **garmin_tomato_clock** — https://github.com/alfonso-orta/garmin_tomato_clock
  Pomodoro clock for Vivoactive 4. Stars: 2.

- **SailingTimer** — https://github.com/spikyjt/SailingTimer
  Sailing start timer. Stars: 9.

- **CPR-Timer** — https://github.com/lukasbeckercode/CPR-Timer
  CPR timing app.

- **garmin_intervals** — https://github.com/celo-vschi/garmin-intervals
  Intervals app for Forerunner 235, 245, and Fenix 6 Pro.

## Not Audio-Related

The remaining 600+ entries are watch faces, data fields, fitness apps, etc.
with no audio/tone functionality.
