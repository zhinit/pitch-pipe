# Forerunner 245/245 Music Owner's Manual — Specifications

Source: https://www8.garmin.com/manuals/webhelp/forerunner245/EN-US/GUID-BE888A02-503B-41E6-8F34-15604E0B9CA3.html
Fetched: 2026-06-14

## Battery Life

- **Watch Mode (245)**: Up to 7 days with activity tracking, smartphone
  notifications, and wrist-based heart rate
- **Activity Mode (245)**: Up to 24 hr with GPS and wrist-based heart rate
- **Watch Mode with Music (245 Music)**: Up to 8 hr with activity tracking,
  smartphone notifications, wrist-based heart rate, and music playback
- **Activity Mode with Music (245 Music)**: Up to 6 hr with GPS, wrist-based
  heart rate, and music playback

## Connectivity and Radio

Both models support ANT+ and Bluetooth 4.2. The Music variant adds Wi-Fi
connectivity.

## Storage

The 245 Music includes "3.5 GB (approximately 500 songs)" media capacity.

## Environmental Specs

- Operating temperature: −20° to 60°C
- Charging temperature: 0° to 45°C
- Water rating: 5 ATM for swimming
